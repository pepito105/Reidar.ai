WHAT WE DO
Finny AI is an AI-native compliance and regulatory automation platform for financial institutions. We replace manual compliance workflows with AI agents that monitor transactions, flag anomalies, generate regulatory filings, and keep financial firms audit-ready in real time.

THE PROBLEM
Financial institutions spend $270B annually on compliance globally. A mid-size bank employs 15-30 compliance officers who spend 70% of their time on manual documentation, report generation, and regulatory filing. One missed filing can result in $10M+ in fines. The process hasn't changed in 20 years.

OUR SOLUTION
Finny AI's compliance agents:
- Monitor transactions in real time against 500+ regulatory rules (SEC, FINRA, FCA, MAS)
- Auto-generate SAR (Suspicious Activity Reports), CTR (Currency Transaction Reports), and regulatory filings
- Maintain a live audit trail with explainability for every decision
- Integrate with core banking systems via API in under 2 weeks

WHY NOW
- Basel IV and DORA regulations taking effect in 2025 dramatically increase reporting requirements
- LLMs now powerful enough to understand regulatory language and generate compliant documentation
- Legacy compliance software (Actimize, Oracle FCCM) costs $500K+/year and requires 6-month implementations
